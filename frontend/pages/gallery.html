Submit Your Photos

Did you attend one of our events? Share your photos with us!

Maximum file size: 5MB per image

Photo Guidelines

  • Images must be in JPG, PNG, or HEIF format
  • Maximum of 5 photos per submission
  • Each photo must be less than 5MB in size
  • Photos should be clear and well-lit
  • Photos must be appropriate and related to university events
  • Avoid photos that prominently feature individuals who haven't given consent

By submitting photos, you confirm that you took these photos yourself or have the right to share them.

What Happens Next?

After submission, our team will review your photos. If approved, they may be featured in our gallery and used for university promotional materials.

We appreciate your contribution to documenting our university's vibrant events!
