About iTeam University

Discover our mission, values, and commitment to excellence in event management education.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, iTeam University has been at the forefront of event management education, bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. What started as a small department has grown into a comprehensive platform for aspiring event planners, managers, and coordinators.

Our institution is built on the belief that events are powerful tools for communication, connection, and change. Through our specialized programs and hands-on approach, we equip students with the skills and experience needed to excel in the dynamic world of event planning and management.

Our Mission

To educate, inspire, and prepare the next generation of event professionals through innovative teaching methods, industry partnerships, and real-world experiences.

We strive to cultivate a collaborative learning environment where creativity, critical thinking, and practical skills come together to shape exceptional event managers who can adapt to the evolving demands of the industry.

Our Vision

To be recognized globally as a premier institution for event management education, setting the standard for excellence and innovation in the field.

We envision a world where our graduates lead the way in transforming how events are conceived, planned, and executed, creating meaningful experiences that have lasting impacts on communities and organizations.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Innovation

We embrace new ideas, technologies, and approaches to continuously improve event management practices.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of teamwork and partnership to create exceptional events and learning experiences.

Inclusivity

We create events and educational opportunities that welcome and represent diverse perspectives and backgrounds.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in everything we do, from classroom instruction to event execution.

Sustainability

Integrity
