Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is Student Event Platform?

Student Event Platform is a centralized platform that helps university students discover, join, and organize campus events. Our mission is to enhance campus life by making it easier for students to connect through meaningful events and activities.

Do I need to create an account to use this platform?

Yes, you need to create an account to register for events or organize your own events. However, you can browse available events without logging in.

Is this platform only for students?

While primarily designed for students, faculty members and university staff can also create accounts to organize or participate in campus events.

Event Registration

How do I register for an event?

To register for an event, navigate to the event details page and click the "Register Now" button. You'll need to be logged in to complete the registration.

Can I cancel my registration?

Yes, you can cancel your registration up until the event starts. Go to "My Registrations" in your account dashboard and click the "Cancel Registration" button for the specific event.

Will I receive a confirmation after registering?

Yes, you'll receive an email confirmation once your registration is complete. Some events may require approval from organizers, in which case you'll receive a pending notification until your registration is approved.

Organizing Events

Can I create my own event?

Yes! Student organizations and approved students can create and manage events. Go to your dashboard and click on "Create Event" to get started.

What information do I need to provide when creating an event?

You'll need to provide the event title, description, date and time, location, category, maximum capacity (if applicable), and an image for the event banner.

How can I manage registrations for my event?

As an event organizer, you can view and manage all registrations from your event dashboard. You can approve/deny registrations, download the attendee list, and send updates to registered participants.

Technical Support

I'm having technical issues. How can I get help?

For technical support, please email us at support@studenteventplatform.com with details about the issue you're experiencing. Our team typically responds within 24 hours.

Can I use the platform on my mobile device?

Yes, our platform is fully responsive and works on desktop computers, tablets, and mobile phones.

Does the platform work with calendar applications?

Yes, you can add events to your Google Calendar, Outlook, or download an iCal file from any event's details page by clicking the "Add to Calendar" button.
