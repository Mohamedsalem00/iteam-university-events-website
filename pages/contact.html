Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I register for an event?

You can register for events through our Registration page. Simply select the event you wish to attend, fill out the form, and submit your registration.

Are events open to non-students?

Most events are open to the public, though some may have priority registration for university students and faculty. Check the specific event details for more information.

How can I become an event sponsor?

For sponsorship opportunities, please contact our partnerships team at partnerships@iteamuniversity.edu with details about your organization.

Can I propose an event idea?

Yes! We welcome event suggestions. Please fill out the contact form above and select "Other" as the subject, then describe your event idea in detail.
